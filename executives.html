Executives

In the college media streaming system, musically based offenses are considered especially heinous. In Granville Ohio, the dedicated DJs who run the show are members of an elite squad known as the Doobie Executive Board. These are their profiles. Dun dun.

Olivia Vrablik (‘22)
Station Manager

Olivia is a senior studying Cinema. Her favorite genres include new wave, hip-hop, alternative rock, and electronic pop but she’s always down to pull out some 2000s hits. Tune into her show “Lego Star Wars Cantina” on Fridays from 9:30-11:30.

Betsy Wagner (‘23)
Art and Public Relations Director

Betsy is a junior who's studying art history and economics. She is the host of “FARE ENUF” which highlights a hodgepodge of music and “insightful” commentary from friends and foes of Betsy. Each show fluctuates depending on how much new music is brewed up and what controversial topics are brought to the table to be debated. No matter what, though, no guest of the show can leave without FARE ENUF tattooed on their knuckles.

Kat Amiet (‘23)
Program Coordinator

Hey y'all :) Kat is a junior studying Cinema and International Studies. The Doobie was the reason that Kat applied to Denison and they have been a DJ since freshman year. They host The Show Around The Corner, a two hour music + talk show with a theme that changes every week. If you listen in to the show, you're likely to hear them talking about The OC, Gossip Girl or any new music releases of the week.

Meredith ‘Dith’ Ehlers (‘22)
Finance Director

Meredith is a senior studying psychology and education. She is 50% of the Slutty Shark Rodeo radio show co-hosted by Jack May. Tune in for an eclectic mix of sad indie, prog rock, witty banter, and so much more. This is her fifth semester as a DJ and second semester as finance director.
